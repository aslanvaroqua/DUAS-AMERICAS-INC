Our history

DUAS Americas was founded along Florida's gold coast in the City of Boca Raton. The city, once the home to tech-giant IBM, remains a center for STEM industries, and a hotbed for startup activity and entrepreurship. Co-founder & CEO Aslan Varoqua spent years in South Florida's startup scene. It was this experience working in small teams which allowed him to really get a feel for the business behind the technology. Duas Americas Group Inc became a for-profit c-corporation after filing and receiving subsequent approval of the Articles of Incorporation with the State of Florida and rec.
